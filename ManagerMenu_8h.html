The ManagerMenu module provides the interactive interface for zoo managers. It allows management of animals, aviaries, and employees under the manager’s supervision, as well as general system monitoring operations.

The ManagerMenu serves as a mid-level administrative control panel that complements the higher-privileged AdminMenu. It enables managers to:

  • View and feed animals;
  • Assign or reassign employees;
  • Manage aviaries and occupancy;
  • Review zoo structure and paths.
